# **App Name**: Boundless Books

## Core Features:

- Service Information: Provide detailed descriptions of available book binding services and options.
- File Upload: Enable users to upload their documents (PDF, DOC, images) for binding.
- Binding Customization: Offer customizable binding options such as paper type, cover material, and binding style.
- Instant Price Estimation: Generate instant price estimates based on selected options, utilizing an AI-powered tool to refine the prices according to real-time availability.
- Order Placement & Payment: Allow users to place orders and make payments through a secure payment gateway.
- Order Tracking: Provide a system for users to track the status of their orders.
- Contact & Support: Offer a contact form and support resources for user inquiries.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep indigo (#3F51B5) to convey professionalism and trust, reflecting the scholarly nature of books.
- Background color: Very light gray (#F0F0F0) to provide a clean and neutral backdrop that enhances readability and reduces visual fatigue.
- Accent color: Muted violet (#9575CD) to add a touch of creativity and highlight key interactive elements such as buttons and links.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', serif, for headlines or small amounts of text; body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, if longer text is anticipated
- Use simple, professional icons related to books and binding processes.
- Maintain a clean and organized layout to ensure ease of navigation.
- Subtle animations on button hovers and page transitions to enhance user experience.
